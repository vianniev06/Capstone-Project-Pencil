About Me

Hello, my name is Viannie Veras and am currently a high school student. I currently attend Inwood Early
College for Health and Information Technologies. I dedicated time to learning the importance of financial literacy
to better guarentee financial success in my future. As I am learning about the world of finance, I want to share
my learning experience with other teenagers and young adults, so that they can also be better equipped to
deal with any obstacles that come from a lack of financial literacy.
